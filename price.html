ABOUT

Silverado Cinema is one of the leading cinemas in Australia. We offer movies in both 2d and 3d, and have the latest dolbi digital and surround sound system installed in all of our cinemas, for you enjoyment. We also offer purchasing of ticket both online and on-site, with a variety of payment methods to choose from. Relax in our new refurbished facility, with brand new seats, screens, and projectors. We have a rich history of providing great service, to both our previous customers and out new ones.
